Back Projects

Nafez allows you to support entrepreneurs and creatives by pledging funds to their campaigns. As a backer you’ll help bring ideas to life and receive exclusive rewards in return.

  • Explore projects and discover inspiring stories.
  • Select a reward tier that matches your contribution.
  • Track progress and celebrate success with your creators.

Fund Your Ideas

As a seeker you can raise the capital you need to prototype, build and scale. Share your vision, set your funding goal and let the community propel you forward.

  • Create a compelling campaign story with photos and videos.
  • Set funding targets and offer attractive reward tiers.
  • Engage with your backers through updates and comments.

Grow Together

Nafez fosters a thriving community of innovators and supporters. Through collaboration, both seekers and backers grow together—sharing knowledge, resources and opportunities.

  • Network with other entrepreneurs and industry experts.
  • Receive mentorship through our Seekers+ program.
  • Unlock new markets and partnerships as your project succeeds.
