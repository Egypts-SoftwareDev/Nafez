How Does It Work?

We connect entrepreneurs with businesses or ideas [Seekers] to opportunists that are willing to back them [Backers].

No Risk. Just Reward.

If a campaign isn't fully funded in time, then backers get their money back. Your support always matters, and you never lose out.

Prepare to Succeed

Seekers can use our Seekers+ plan as well as our free resource center to ensure that their campaign is ready to receive funding.

Backers Manual

Backing a project on Nafez is simple and rewarding. Follow these steps to discover great ideas and support the entrepreneurs behind them.

  1. Browse through different campaigns on your feed.
  2. Select a campaign to view its details and story.
  3. Choose the reward tier that suits you best.
  4. Complete the payment process using our secure checkout.
  5. Wait and track your backed campaign's progress until the deadline.
  6. At the deadline: if the campaign is fully funded, celebrate with the creator and receive your rewards. If it isn't funded, your pledge is refunded automatically.
  7. Leave a review and share your experience with others.
  8. Opt in to get notified if the campaign restarts.

Seekers Manual

Turning your idea into a successful campaign requires preparation and support. Here's how to get started on Nafez.

  1. Fill in the application to register as a Seeker.
  2. Submit your strategy, business plan and campaign post.
  3. Undergo a feedback round with the Nafez team.
  4. Get approved and sign the agreement at the Nafez booth.
  5. Your campaign goes live! Share it with your network.
  6. Collect funds from backers until your chosen deadline.
  7. At the deadline: if fully funded, provide proof of fulfilment. Once verified, funds are transferred to you.
  8. If your campaign doesn't reach its goal, contact our Seekers+ program to refine your idea and try again. All pledges are refunded to backers.
  9. Successful projects earn a trust badge and move up the leaderboard.
